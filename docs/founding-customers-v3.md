# Founding Customers v3 — v1.0.1

**Program limit:** 10 slots · **Discount:** 50% beta pricing

## Offer summary

First 10 agencies receive lifetime founding benefits: beta pricing, priority support, roadmap influence, and founding badge.

## Acquisition funnel

```
Outbound list → Enriched lead → Discovery call → Pilot application → Founding enrollment → Customer success
```

## v3 changes (Sprint 2)

- Outbound workspace with six segment lists
- Lead enrichment and priority scoring
- Template library for every conversion stage
- Automation: cadence, aging, no-response, escalation
- Customer success dashboard with renewal probability

## Enrollment

1. Qualify lead in CRM (`qualified` or `pilot_application`).
2. Run discovery call and proposal.
3. Enroll via lead detail → **Enroll as founding customer**.
4. Track onboarding in `/sales/success`.

## Success criteria

- 10 founding customers enrolled
- ≥ 8/10 complete onboarding checklist
- ≥ 70% average renewal probability at day 45
