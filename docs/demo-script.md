# Demo Script

## Prep (5 min)

- Open `/dashboard` demo workspace
- Confirm `/status` is operational
- Have pilot program page ready: `/pilot-program`

## Introduction (5 min)

1. Problem: agencies juggle clients across tools without one command center
2. Positioning: Auroranexis = operations layer for AI automation agencies
3. Pilot offer: 6 weeks, 50% beta pricing, founding customer benefits

## Product walkthrough (20 min)

1. **Dashboard** — workspace health, alerts, quick actions
2. **Clients** — portfolio health and delivery context
3. **Reports** — template → draft → publish → portal
4. **Automation** — workflow overview (no deep builder unless asked)
5. **Compliance** — GDPR tooling and audit posture (readiness, not certification claims)
6. **Settings / Diagnostics** — production and pilot readiness scores

## Trust surfaces (5 min)

- `/security`, `/compliance`, `/privacy`
- `/status` and support at `/support`

## Close (5 min)

- Ask for pilot fit confirmation
- CTA: email `sales@auroranexis.com` with agency profile
- Share `/pilot-program` link

## Do not claim

- SOC 2, ISO 27001, or other certifications unless formally obtained
