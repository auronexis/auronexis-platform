# Customer Objections

## "We already use a PSA / RMM."

**Response:** Auroranexis complements operational tools with client-facing reporting, risk/incident workflows, automation orchestration, and a unified command center — not a replacement for every PSA feature in pilot phase.

## "We are not ready for another SaaS."

**Response:** Pilot is 6 weeks with dedicated onboarding and 50% beta pricing. Start with reporting and one client portal workflow before expanding.

## "Where is data hosted?"

**Response:** EU-capable infrastructure (Supabase, Vercel). Specific residency confirmed during enterprise onboarding. See `/privacy` and `/subprocessors`.

## "Do you have enterprise certifications?"

**Response:** We map to SOC 2 / ISO 27001 readiness but do not claim certifications we have not obtained. See `/compliance`.

## "What if the product is early?"

**Response:** That is why we run a structured pilot — roadmap influence and founding customer pricing for partners who co-shape the platform.

## "Security concerns"

**Response:** Share `/security-policy`, security contact `security@auroranexis.com`, and diagnostics-backed `/status` page.
