# Discovery Call Script

**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Goal:** Qualify fit + schedule demo

---

## Opening (2 min)

> "Thanks for your interest in the Auroranexis founding pilot. I'll ask a few questions about your agency, then show how the platform fits — sound good?"

---

## Qualification (10 min)

1. **Agency profile** — How many active clients? Team size?
2. **Stack** — PSA/RMM, automation tools, reporting today?
3. **Pain** — Where do clients lack visibility? (reports, incidents, portal)
4. **AI usage** — Delivering AI automation to clients? How documented?
5. **Timeline** — Looking to improve operations in next 90 days?
6. **Decision** — Who else needs to see a demo?

**Disqualify gently if:** < 3 clients, no operational delivery, no budget authority.

---

## Positioning (5 min)

- Auroranexis = **operations command center** for AI automation agencies
- Not replacing PSA — complementing with client-facing delivery
- Pilot: 6 weeks, 50% off, founding benefits

---

## Demo offer (3 min)

> "I'd like to show you our staging demo with realistic data — Acme Automation as a sample client. 35 minutes. Does {{day}} work?"

Send: [pilot-invitation.md](./pilot-invitation.md) + `/pilot-program` link

---

## Close (2 min)

- Confirm email for calendar invite
- Mention legal pages: `/privacy`, `/data-processing-agreement`
- Next: demo using [demo-script-v2.md](../demo-script-v2.md)
