# Objection Handling — Pilot Sales

Quick responses for discovery and demo calls. See also [customer-objections.md](../customer-objections.md).

---

## "We already have a PSA."

**Response:** Auroranexis complements your PSA with client-facing reports, portal, risk/incident workflows, and automation orchestration — start with one client workflow in the pilot without migrating your PSA.

---

## "Too early / too risky."

**Response:** The 6-week pilot is designed for early adopters — 50% pricing, weekly support, and you can export data and leave with no long-term lock-in.

---

## "We need SOC 2."

**Response:** We map to SOC 2 controls and publish our security policy, but we don't claim certifications we haven't obtained. Happy to share our compliance roadmap and DPA.

---

## "Our clients won't use another portal."

**Response:** Portal is optional — many agencies start with published PDF reports and add portal when clients ask for self-service. White label branding keeps your agency front and center.

---

## "Price after pilot?"

**Response:** Professional is €249/mo; founding pilots get 50% off for 6 months. At one client saved hour per week, the ROI typically clears in month one.

---

## "Can we white label?"

**Response:** Yes — Business plan and above include white label; demo workspace shows published branding on staging.

---

## "What about integrations?"

**Response:** 13 OAuth connectors (Google, Slack, GitHub, etc.). Pilot focuses on 2–3 connectors you actually use — we register staging callbacks together in week 1.

---

## "Need EU data residency."

**Response:** Infrastructure on Supabase/Vercel with EU-capable deployment; specific residency terms in enterprise DPA during conversion.
