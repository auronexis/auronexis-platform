# First 10 Customers Plan

**Version:** v1.0.0  
**Target:** 10 founding customers  
**Status:** Revenue Generation

## Cohort definition

Founding customers are the first **10 agencies** enrolled via the founding program. Each receives:

- 50% pilot pricing
- Lifetime subscription discount
- Founding badge
- Roadmap influence
- Priority support

## Acquisition funnel

```
Marketing site → Lead capture → Discovery → Proposal → Pilot → Paid subscription
```

## Weekly targets (Sprint 1)

| Week | Goal |
|------|------|
| 1 | Pipeline live, forms capturing leads |
| 2 | 5+ discovery calls scheduled |
| 3 | 3+ proposals sent |
| 4 | First founding enrollment |

## ICP

- MSPs and AI automation agencies
- 5–50 employees
- Managing multiple client operations
- Pain: visibility, reporting, risk detection

## Tracking

- `/sales` dashboard — pipeline counts and MRR
- Founding slots: `{enrolled}/10`
- Revenue readiness diagnostics ≥ 99

## Conversion checklist per customer

- [ ] Pilot application received
- [ ] Discovery call completed
- [ ] Proposal sent
- [ ] Founding slot assigned
- [ ] Workspace created / subscription active
- [ ] Case study template started

## Owner

Sales owner assigned in CRM on qualification.
